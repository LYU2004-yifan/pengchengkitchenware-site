A UK-registered sourcing and supply support company based in London — bridging UK commercial requirements with Chinese manufacturing capability.

What we solve

Many buyers can find factories online — the real challenge is reducing uncertainty: communication gaps, inconsistent quality, unclear lead times, and documentation issues. We provide a structured workflow to make overseas sourcing more controllable.

How we operate

  • London-based point of contact for fast communication
  • Factory matching based on capability and specification
  • Transparent quotation comparison and trade-offs
  • Sampling and production follow-up
  • QC coordination based on buyer requirements
