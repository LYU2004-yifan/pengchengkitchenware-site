How we support your sourcing project

Practical service areas for commercial kitchenware and catering equipment projects.

Requirement review

Review your specification, MOQ, budget, and lead time.

Supplier matching

Identify suitable factories based on capability and export experience.

Sampling support

Quality coordination

Logistics coordination

Support packing, documentation, and shipment coordination to the UK.

Typical engagement workflow

What you can expect when working with us.

  1. Share requirements — drawings/photos, materials, dimensions, finish, and quantity.
  2. We shortlist factories — capability and export experience matched to your needs.
  3. Quotations & options — lead times, trade-offs, and cost structure explained clearly.
  4. Sampling — coordinate samples and revisions where needed.
  5. Production follow-up — updates and problem solving throughout manufacturing.
  6. QC & shipment — coordinate agreed criteria and shipment documentation to the UK.

To start quickly: send your spec + target lead time + estimated order quantity.
